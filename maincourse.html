Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

Cook spaghetti. In a pan, cook pancetta. Whisk eggs and cheese together. Combine everything, adding pasta water to achieve desired consistency.

Grilled Chicken Breast

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Herbs (rosemary, thyme)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

Marinate chicken in olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and herbs. Grill until cooked through. Season with salt and pepper.
