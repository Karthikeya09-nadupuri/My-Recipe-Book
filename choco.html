Decadent Chocolate Truffles

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 100ml heavy cream
  • 30g unsalted butter
  • Cocoa powder for coating

Instructions:

  1. Heat cream in a saucepan until it just begins to simmer.
  2. Pour hot cream over chopped chocolate and let sit for 2 minutes.
  3. Stir until smooth, then add butter and mix well.
  4. Chill mixture for 2 hours.
  5. Shape into small balls and roll in cocoa powder.

Fudgy Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 200g unsalted butter
  • 250g dark chocolate
  • 300g sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 100g all-purpose flour
  • 30g cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Melt butter and chocolate together.
  3. Whisk in sugar and eggs.
  4. Fold in flour and cocoa powder.
  5. Pour into a lined baking tin and bake for 25-30 minutes.

Hot Chocolate Soufflé

Ingredients:

  • 50g unsalted butter
  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 6 egg whites
  • 50g sugar
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Melt butter and chocolate together.
  3. Whisk in egg yolks and sugar.
  4. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  5. Fold egg whites into chocolate mixture.
  6. Pour into buttered ramekins and bake for 12-15 minutes.
