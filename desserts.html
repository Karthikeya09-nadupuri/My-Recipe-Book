Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Baking powder
  • Milk

Instructions:

Preheat the oven. Mix dry ingredients. Add eggs , butter, and milk. Pour into a greased pan and bake until a toothpick comes out clean.

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • Assorted fruits (strawberries, blueberries, grapes, pineapple)
  • Honey
  • Lemon juice

Instructions:

Cut fruits into bite-sized pieces. Mix honey and lemon juice. Combine fruits and honey-lemon mixture. Serve chilled.
